Jeera Rice

This recipe comes from someone I work with - her cooking is fantastic and she shared some of her recipes with me. Jeera is the Hindi word for cumin.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Cumin seeds
  • Cashews (optional)
  • Sultanas (optional)
  • Coriander, finely chopped
  • Salt

Method

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Heat oil, add finely chopped onions, a pinch of salt, cumin seeds, cashews and sultanas.
  3. Fry until onions soften, then add half coriander and fry for a minute.
  4. Stir through cooked rice, then add remaining coriander.
