Potato Fry

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Mustard seeds
  • Urad dal
  • Chenna dal
  • Dried chilli (whole or flakes)
  • Garlic, crushed
  • Turmeric
  • Chilli powder
  • Salt

Method

  1. Cut potatoes into small cubes and cook separately. They can be slightly underdone.
  2. Heat oil, add all ingredients except turmeric, potatoes and chilli powder.
  3. Fry until onions soften.
  4. Stir through potatoes, add turmeric and chilli poweder.

Notes

The recipe

Used black lentils instead of urad dal. Black lentils are too tough when they've only been fried for a short time.

Bridget's original notes on the recipe were unclear about whether the potatoes should be cooked separately, but she did so for another recipe so I'm assuming for now.
