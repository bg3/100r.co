Spectrum PI

A variant of the ZX Spectrum system font I created. The original has a light airy feel, but there are a few awkward characters and some that are hard to distinguish (WV). Some of its quirks (blocky X and period) are its best features though - it seems to me that the typeface was designed to leave a pixel border wherever possible... maybe for showing on inverted background.

You can download a copy as a .chr file for Nasu.
