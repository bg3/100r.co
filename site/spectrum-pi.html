Spectrum PI

This character set is based on the ZX Spectrum system font, but extended to cover the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Code_page_437 ASCII OEM]] character set. The original Spectrum font has a light airy feel, but there are a few awkward characters and some that are hard to distinguish (e.g. W and V). Some of its quirks (blocky X and period) are what give the typeface its character, so I've left those unchanged.

You can download a copy as a [[https://parkimminent.com/media/bin/spectrum-pi.chr .chr file]] for [[https://wiki.xxiivv.com/site/nasu.html Nasu]].
