Japanese notes (full)

の substituting for a noun

A noun can be left out of a sentence where it can be implied.

あれは　だれの　かばんですか。Whose bag is that?
…わたしのですIt's mine.

〜も and 〜か

何も means nothing (or anything), 何か means something. 何も　食べません = "I'm not going to eat anything."
