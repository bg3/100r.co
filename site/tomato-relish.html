Tomato relish

)

Ingredients

  • 5 lb tomatoes
  • 2 lb onions
  • 1 lb sugar
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 1/2 tbsp mustard powder
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2-3 tbsp salt
  • brown vinegar

Method

Chop onions finely. Put tomatoes, onions + sugar in a preserving pan, cover with vinegar. Boil 3/4 hour then mix powders + flour together with a little water and add to pan and boil a few minutes longer.
