## Ingredients

|---: | :---  | :--- |
| 1   | pkt   | Edgell Instant Mashed Potatoes |
| 2   | cups  | boiling water |

## Method
Slowly add boiling water to mashed potato powder while stirring.

Stand for 1 minute.

Sit down and eat it.

### Hot tip
I added some butter and pepper.
