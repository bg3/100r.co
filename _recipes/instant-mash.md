## Ingredients

|---: | :---  | :--- |
| 1   | pkt   | Edgell Instant Mashed Potatoes |
| 200 | ml    | boiling water |

## Method
Slowly add boiling water to mash potato powder while stirring.

Stand for 2 minutes.

Sit down and eat it.
